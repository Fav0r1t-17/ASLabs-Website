TextNormalizer — Download and Installation Notes

Thank you for purchasing TextNormalizer.

Installation:
1. Download the installer from your Gumroad purchase page.
2. Run the installer on a supported Windows system.
3. Follow the on-screen setup instructions.
4. Launch TextNormalizer after installation.

Important notes:
- Windows only.
- No account is required for normal use.
- Internet connection is not required for normal use.
- Large text files may take longer to process depending on file size and content.
- Future updates, if released, may be provided through updated files or product updates.

If you need help, contact:
support@aslabs.dev

When contacting support, include your purchase email, product version, Windows version, and a short description of the issue.
