Refund Policy

TextNormalizer  ·  Last updated: 2026

TextNormalizer is a digital software product delivered as a downloadable installer.

Because digital products can be downloaded and retained after purchase, refunds are not automatically guaranteed. However, refund requests may be considered in limited cases.

1. Eligible refund situations

A refund request may be considered if:

  • the product cannot be installed or launched under supported conditions on a supported Windows system;
  • the delivered file is corrupted, missing, or materially different from the product description;
  • a technical issue prevents basic use of the product and the issue cannot be resolved after reasonable support contact.

2. Non-refundable situations

Refunds will generally not be provided for:

  • change of mind after purchase;
  • unsupported operating systems or unsupported environments;
  • requests based on planned features that are not part of the current released version;
  • issues caused by third-party software, local system configuration, antivirus interference, or user environment where the product itself is not defective;
  • failure to read the product description, supported platform information, or current feature list before purchase.

3. How to request a refund

To request a refund, contact: support@aslabs.dev

Please include:

  • your purchase email;
  • your order details;
  • your Windows version;
  • a short description of the problem;
  • and, if possible, a screenshot or error message.

4. Review process

Refund requests are reviewed individually. If a technical issue can be resolved, support may be offered before a refund decision is made.

5. Platform rules

Sales may also be subject to Gumroad platform rules and payment provider requirements. Gumroad may issue refunds in certain situations under its platform policies.
